Bikroy — Manual Test Case Writing Project

website: https://bikroy.com/

Project Overview
This is a manual Software Quality Assurance (SQA) project for Bikroy, one of Bangladesh's largest online classifieds and marketplace platforms. 
The purpose of this project is to manually design and document test cases that validate the correctness, usability, and reliability of the user-facing flows on the platform. 
The work covers the Sign Up, Sign In, and Account Management modules, as well as the Post Ad functionality across all available product categories.
Application Under Test
Bikroy (https://www.bikroy.com) is an online platform in Bangladesh where users can register, log in, and post advertisements to buy and sell products across a wide range of categories including electronics, vehicles, fashion, property, and more.
Scope of Testing
The test cases written in this project target two main areas. The first is the User Management module, which includes the Sign Up flow, the Sign In flow, and Account Management features. The second is the Post Ad functionality, which covers all fourteen product categories available on the platform — Mobiles, Electronics, Vehicles, Home and Living, Pets and Animals, Property, Women's Fashion and Beauty, Men's Fashion and Grooming, Hobbies Sports and Kids, Business and Industry, Essentials, Education, Services, and Agriculture.
